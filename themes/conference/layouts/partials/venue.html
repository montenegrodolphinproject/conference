Venue

The mountainous landscape and picturesque coastline of southern Montenegro provide an ideal backdrop for this event. Ulcinj is a small town with pine forests, and rocky and sandy beaches, which are perfect for both adventure activities and relaxation. The conference will be held at the Albatros Hotel, located less than 1 km from the center of town.
